Workspace landing page

One of the challenges of traveling so frequently is finding a nice quiet place to get some work done. Sure, the hotel is an obvious choice, but it isn't always an option. A bar? Sure, but not always ideal either. Library? What are their hours? Is wifi available? Is it conducive to working or more of a community center?

There's got to be a better way than hit or miss. Yelp? Doesn't really cover it. Are there tables without edges so you can use a mouse? Are the surfaces reflective and you need a mousepad? Little things can make you less productive, even get annoying... let's work happy!

This app helps the traveler find that perfect place. It relies on other business travelers who may have found a great place, and allows them to rate, review establishments on a business-friendly environment point of view.

Want a place with a view? Or maybe a place that allows smoking? Yup... I present to you, 'Workspace'
