Workspace landing page

One of the challenges of traveling so frequently is finding a nice quiet place to get some work done. Sure, the hotel is an obvious choice, but if you travel as much as I do, the idea of locking yourself up in a hotel room all day/night isn't very appealing. Problem is, where can you go? A bar? Well, does it have wifi? Is it loud? What about the public library? What are the hours? Parking available? Wifi? is it conducive to working or more of a community center?

Finding a good place to work that has all you are looking for is like heaven on earth. This app helps the traveler who needs to spread out, or maybe just wants a place they can smoke. How about a restaurant with wifi, or TVs to catch the game while answering emails. Work with a view? See where I am going with this? Help your fellow traveling business-folk, leave a tip, take a tip.

I present to you, 'Workspace'
